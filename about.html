About us

OopslizdidmyTips was founded in late December 2024 as a hobby by Jessica, shortly after completing her matric.
Inspired by her own experience having her nails done for her Matric dance, Jessica began learning nail design and creation.
Although the process was challenging and definetly not impossible, she remained committed to refining her skills and experimenting with new designs.
After many failed attempts in trying to master acrylic techniques, OopslizdidmyTips officially launched in May 2025. Jessica gained most of her experience working with family members and now plans to become a certified nail technician.

>The VISION

To learn more and new Techniques within the industry, while doing so in a productive and sanitary manner.
We plan to become a reputable business that teaches others how to do nails and promote other up-coming nail technicians and other creatives within the beauty space.

The MISSION

To empower people who love doing their nails by allowing the creative autonomy that they deserve and delivering that.
We want to make everyone feel good and confident in themselves, even if it’s just by doing their nails.

Our Team

Currently, there is only one person on the team, which is Jessica.
